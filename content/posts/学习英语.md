### Helping verbs (verbs can help other verbs )

what helping verbs are is really just inflected forms of the verbs to have , and to be .

eg.

Brian is eating a pizza .

Brian has eaten a pizza .

Brian was eating a pizza .

Brain had been eating a pizza .

### Model verbs (help other verbs )

Modality is when we use these verbs to express conditions on stuff .

- **they agree with everything** . you can use any subject in the world and it's not gonna change .
- they don't have a “to ”form , modals don't have infinitives.
- modal verbs can do is indicate modality .

### The vowel -shift irregular verb

`vowel` a vowel is any sound that your mouth can make while your tongue isn't touching your lips or your teeth or the roof of your mouth.

you produce vowels from different parts of your mouth .

it goes from front , to middle , to back as you go further and further back in time.
